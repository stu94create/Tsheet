How to Use This

Adding entries during the week

Tap "Add Entry" on the main screen. You'll go through 3 steps: pick a day, pick or type a task type, then fill in the details and hours. Tap "Save Entry" when you're done. You can add as many entries as you need throughout the week.

Editing or deleting

Each entry has Edit and Delete links underneath it. Edit takes you back to the details step so you can change anything. Delete removes it straight away.

The summary

The summary shows your total hours, how many are claimed (capped at your max — set this in Settings), and any time owing. "Time Owed" entries are subtracted before calculating.

Custom tasks

On the task step, pick a built-in type or type your own. Custom tasks are remembered and appear as buttons next time.

Exporting for Excel

At the end of the week, tap "Export for Excel." This downloads a CSV file that opens in Excel and Numbers.

Opening the CSV in Excel on iPhone

After it downloads, open the Files app and find it in Downloads. Tap the file to preview it. Tap the Share button (the square with the arrow), then choose "Copy to Excel" or "Open in Excel."

Emailing from Excel on iPhone

With the file open in Excel, tap the three-dot menu at the top right, then "Send a Copy." Choose "Email" and pick Excel workbook as the format. This opens a new email with the timesheet attached. Add the address, a subject, and tap Send.

Emailing from Numbers on iPhone

In Numbers, tap the three-dot menu, then "Export," then "Excel." Tap Share, then Mail. This sends it as an Excel file.

Settings

In Settings you can change your maximum weekly hours. The default is 7.
